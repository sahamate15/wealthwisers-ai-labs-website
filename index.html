What "digital employees" actually means

Digital employees. Human decisions.

Think of Samriddhi's agents as analysts you hire, not software you replace people with. They take the heavy, repetitive analytical work off your team's plate, so the people who know your clients spend their time deciding, not assembling.

The agents do

The analytical grunt work.

  • Read statements and holdings directly, with no manual data entry.
  • Build the full portfolio picture and run the diagnostics.
  • Surface what moved, what drifted, and what needs a closer look, each with its reason.
  • Turn hours of prep into minutes, so reviews and onboarding keep pace with your book.
Your people keep

The relationship and the judgment.

  • The client relationship, the conversation, and the trust.
  • The judgment about what a finding means for this client.
  • The final call, and the accountability that comes with it.
  • Decision support, never advice. Nothing here tells anyone what to buy.

Why firms choose us

One secure environment. Yours alone.

Two locks on the same door. Your data stays inside your own deployment, and even inside it, personal information is protected before the AI ever sees what it does not need to.

Isolation

Your firm's own deployment.

Samriddhi runs as one secure cloud environment for your firm alone. Your client data never leaves it and never mixes with anyone else's. Zero pooling, zero leakage.

Sanitisation

PII protected before the AI sees it.

A proprietary data-sanitisation engine strips and protects personally identifiable information in line with DPDP requirements, before the agents ever process anything they do not need to see.

Together: your data stays yours, and within that, PII is sanitised for DPDP compliance. The deployment and the agents are tuned to your firm, not a one-size-fits-all SaaS.

Ready, not scrambling

As the rules on AI tighten, you are already aligned.

DPDP by design

Privacy built in, not bolted on.

Because PII is sanitised before processing and your data stays in your own environment, DPDP alignment follows from how the system is built, not from a policy added later.

SEBI readiness

Ahead of the AI disclosure curve.

SEBI has issued a Consultation Paper on guidelines for responsible usage of AI/ML in Indian Securities Markets (20 June 2025). As the regulator moves on AI usage disclosure, firms on Samriddhi are positioned to be ready rather than scrambling.

Open source

Built with IMT CFM, and open to inspection.

Samriddhi is an open-source project, built in partnership with the Centre for Financial Markets at IMT Ghaziabad (IMT CFM). Open source does double duty here: it brings academic rigour to the work, and it means a firm can verify what the system actually does, rather than take our word for it.
