What it does

Two jobs, one standard of proof.

Flagship capability

Portfolio diagnostics

Continuous health checks across a book of clients: concentration, drift from mandate, suitability flags, and risk-band breaches, each surfaced with its evidence and ready for the advisor's judgment. The advisor sees what moved, why it matters, and the rule it touches, not a black-box score.

Severity vocabulary: escalate, flag, pass. Evidence attached to every finding.View the walkthrough
Strong second

Proposal evaluation

Evaluate new PMS and AIF proposals against the client's mandate and risk profile, with a structured, evidenced read the advisor can defend. The question shifts from "does this product look good" to "can this product be justified for this client, on the record."

Instrument codes in monospace: pms_growth_quality, mf_active_large_cap.View the walkthrough

The EGA framework, in the product

Built to be questioned.

Samriddhi is the EGA framework in working form: the honest pitch and the compliant one at the same time. The advisor stays in charge, and the work is defensible when SEBI, a client, or a committee asks how it was reached.

Governance
